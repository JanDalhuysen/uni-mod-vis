# 50040 Computer Skills

## First Year

### 136 (4) Computer skills and digital literacies (1L)

This module fosters essential digital literacies, equipping students with the critical understanding and
practical skills necessary to navigate and utilise technology effectively. Beyond basic computer skills, we
delve into the core principles of digital citizenship, emphasizing ethical and values-based practices and
ensuring your safety and responsible behaviour in the online world. You will learn to leverage the digital
systems provided by Stellenbosch University to enhance your learning journey, mastering the tools
necessary to manage your digital learning environment with confidence. This includes developing
proficiency in word processing, spreadsheet analysis and creating compelling presentations, all while
adhering to the standards of scientific reporting and data management. The module emphasises the
importance of professional communication and collaboration in digital spaces, preparing you to effectively
contribute to and engage within online learning communities. Ultimately, this module aims to cultivate
digitally literate, responsible and proficient scholars who can confidently utilise technology to support their
academic success and future professional endeavours.
