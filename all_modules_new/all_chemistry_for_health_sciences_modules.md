# 65692 Chemistry for Health Sciences

## First Year

### 112 (8) Chemistry for Health Sciences (2L, 1T)

The module covers areas of general chemistry required as a foundation for studying further in the health
science focal areas of physiotherapy and dietetics. It comprises atomic structure and bonding; stoichiometry;
gas laws; properties of solutions; chemical kinetics and equilibria; organic chemistry and biomolecules.
