# 13080 Physiology

## Second Year

### 214 (16) Physiological Principles and Systems (3L, 3P)

The module provides a comprehensive overview of various human physiological principles and systems,
including homeostasis and body fluids, cell membranes, general physiological control systems,
neurophysiology, special senses, endocrinology, and the digestive system. It also covers typical diseases
related to these systems.

For the Human Life Sciences programme, and the interdisciplinary focal area Applied Medicinal Chemistry:

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biology 124, 154

**Corequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214

For the interdisciplinary focal area Biomedical Mathematical Sciences:

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biology 124

**Corequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214

### 244 (16) Systems in Physiology (3L, 3P)

A comprehensive overview of the physiological systems of the human body, including the muscle and
respiratory systems, acid/base balance, immunology, haematology, urinary, and reproductive systems.

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Physiology 214

**Corequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 244

## Third Year

### 314 (16) Integrated Physiology (3L, 3P)

This module primarily focusses on integrated human physiology and explore a range of topics, including
systems physiology and homeostasis, endocrinology and metabolism, as well as cardiovascular physiology
and neurophysiology.

**Prerequisite pass modules:**
- Physiology 214, 244

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214, 244

### 334 (16) Metabolic Physiology (3L, 3P)

In this module, students explore metabolic physiology including macronutrient digestion, absorption, and
utilisation, inherited diseases of carbohydrate, protein and fat metabolism, and the glycaemic index.

**Prerequisite pass modules:**
- Physiology 214, 244

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214, 244

### 344 (16) Cellular Physiology (3L, 3P)

This module primarily focusses on cellular physiology and explore various topics, including basic principles
of human nutrition, membrane and cell communication, oxidative stress, as well as muscle satellite cells and

stem cells.

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214, 244
- Physiology 314

### 364 (16) Clinical Applied Physiology (3L, 3P)

This module will primarily focus on lifestyle-related diseases and wellness promotion. It will cover the
physiological and cellular aspects of several chronic conditions, including cancer, diabetes, bone physiology
and pathophysiology, atrophy, ageing and infectious diseases.

**Prerequisite pass modules:**
- Physiology 214, 244

**Prerequisite modules:**
- Biochemistry 214, 244

**Physiology as a major for the BSc degree**

The following modules are required: Physiology 214(16), 244(16) 314(16), 334(16), 344(16) and 364(16).
