## Third Year

### 312 (8) Introduction to Bioinformatics (3L, 3P)

Introduction to bioinformatics topics, including biological databases, sequence alignment, dynamic
programming, scoring matrices, BLAST, hidden Markov models (HMMs), phylogenetics, tree building
methods, genome organisation and evolution, genome projects and browsers, evolution of proteins,
classification of protein structures.
